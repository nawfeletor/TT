What Nawfy does

Everything Spotify won't.

⬇
Download Your Music
Right-click any track → Download audio. Saves as m4a/mp3 to your disk. You own it. Spotify will never do this.
Only Nawfy
🎛️
8-Band Equalizer
Real Web Audio API equalizer with 8 frequency bands and presets: Bass, Vocal, Rock, Classical. Spotify has zero EQ.
Only Nawfy
🎬
Cinema Mode
Full-screen album art with animated visualizer and synced lyrics floating beneath. Press P. Spotify has nothing like this.
Only Nawfy
⚡
Smart Playlists
Create playlists that auto-populate by rules: liked tracks, play count, artist, added date. Always up to date.
Only Nawfy
☽
Mood Presets
One click sets your EQ, speed, crossfade and normalize together. Chill, Focus, Energy, Party, Sleep — instant vibe.
Only Nawfy
📝
Track Notes
Attach personal notes to any track — timestamps, chord changes, thoughts. Persisted in your library. Spotify: never.
Only Nawfy
⏩
Playback Speed
7 speed steps from 0.5× to 2×. Keyboard shortcuts + / − to step through. Position resume for podcasts and long mixes.
Nawfy does it better
🎵
Synced Lyrics
Timestamped lyrics from lrclib.net. Auto-scroll, click to seek, show in player bar, show in Cinema mode. Google Translate link.
Nawfy does it better
⏰
Alarm + Sleep Timer
Set an alarm to wake up to a specific track. Set a sleep timer with a visual countdown. Spotify has neither.

Simple setup

Playing in three steps.

I
Install
Download the installer for your OS. Run it. Windows gets a desktop shortcut, Linux gets an AppImage or deb.
II
Get yt-dlp
On first launch, click the one-time setup button. Nawfy downloads yt-dlp automatically (~18 MB). Never again after that.
III
Search & Listen
Search any song directly. Click to add, double-click to play. Your music, your library, forever.
